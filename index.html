ABOUT US

WE BELIEVE IN THE POWER OF INFORMATION TO HELP END POVERTY.

Moriah Resource Centre (MOREC) is a local community based organization,registered under the social and gender department office in Homa Bay County. MOREC's focus is on establishment of higher learning institution.'

GOAL OF THE PROJECT

The goal of the project is to improve information communication and technology needs to residents of Rural Kenya Counties and enable them be productive contributors to their economic growth.

Objectives of MOREC

  • Introduce technical training institutions to serve the rural population in Homa Bay County
  • offer courses in ICT and other disciplines within a walking distance from their homes
  • Enhace technical skills for job creation and employerbility of the population served
  • Introduce online marketing skills that will see youths and job seekers learn
    • Digital skills that will boost their employerability
    • How to build an online presence and create content
    • Web design and User Experience
    • How to leverage on social media
